## 1. Introduction
**"Agriculture is the backbone of our nation, but our farmers are fighting a silent war against pests, diseases, and lack of information."**

Prakriti is a revolutionary **AI-powered agricultural assistant** designed to bridge the gap between advanced technology and the rural farmer. It transforms a simple smartphone into a powerful diagnostic tool, a community center, and an expert advisor.

---

## 2. Problem Statement
Farmers in rural India face three critical challenges:
1.  **Delayed Diagnosis**: By the time a disease is identified, it's often too late to save the crop.
2.  **Knowledge Gap**: Lack of access to immediate, scientific advice in their local language.
3.  **Isolation**: Inability to connect with nearby farmers to share resources or warnings about local pest outbreaks.

*Current solutions are either too expensive, too complex, or require high-speed internet‚Äîleaving the small-scale farmer behind.*

---

## 3. Solution
**Prakriti** offers a unified platform that solves these problems with a "Mobile-First, AI-First" approach:
*   **Instant Diagnosis**: Snap a photo, get a diagnosis in seconds using computer vision.
*   **Voice-First Interface**: Speak to the app in **Hindi, English, or Hinglish**. No typing needed.
*   **Hyper-Local Community**: Automatically find and chat with farmers within a **50km radius**.

---

## 4. Tech Stack
Built for speed, scale, and low connectivity.

*   **Frontend**: React + Vite (Fast, responsive mobile web)
*   **Styling**: TailwindCSS (Lightweight, modern UI)
*   **Backend**: Python FastAPI (High-performance API)
*   **Database**: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Real-time capabilities)
*   **AI Engine**: Google Gemini 1.5 Flash (Multimodal: Vision + Language)
*   **Geolocation**: Browser API + Geospatial Queries (Haversine Formula)

## 6. Feasibility and Challenges
### Feasibility
*   **Technical**: Built on proven, scalable technologies (Google Gemini, Supabase).
*   **Operational**: "Lightweight" web app ensures it works on budget smartphones.
*   **Cost**: Uses cost-effective pay-as-you-go cloud models.

### Challenges & Mitigations
*   **Challenge**: Poor Internet Connectivity.
    *   *Mitigation*: Optimized image compression and text-based fallbacks.
*   **Challenge**: Low Digital Literacy.
    *   *Mitigation*: Voice-first interaction and simple, icon-heavy UI.
*   **Challenge**: Trust in AI.
    *   *Mitigation*: Community validation‚Äîfarmers trust other farmers.

## 7. Sustainable Business Model
How do we sustain and grow?

1.  **Freemium Model**:
    *   **Free**: Basic diagnosis, community access, and AI Chat (limited).
    *   **Premium**: Advanced soil analytics, unlimited AI consultant, and weather alerts.
2.  **Marketplace Commission** (Future):
    *   Small fee on transactions for seeds, fertilizers, and tools sold through the platform.
3.  **Data Insights for B2B**:
    *   Anonymized data on regional pest outbreaks sold to agri-inputs companies and insurers for better supply chain planning.
